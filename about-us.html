About Us

Team SFW

Team-sfw was founded in 2015 by a group of students who take an interest in the aerial vehicle field. We are dedicated to the designing and controlling of aerial robotics, and our current research priorities are drone custom solutions, development of FPV drones, as well as the designing and manufacturing of drones components. Team-sfw is based in Toronto, Canada, we aim to become a team that is technologically independent and has our own independent intellectual property rights of core technology as well as a well-developed international networking. Meanwhile, Team-sfw is also an active participant in MultiGP Drone Racing League because we are very willing to help the drone’s communities. By communicating and collaborating with them, we also get to have a better understanding of the real needs in this industry, thus achieving our goal of utilizing technology to solve daily issues.
